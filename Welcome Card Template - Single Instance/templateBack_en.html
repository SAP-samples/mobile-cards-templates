Welcome to the back of the card!

The back of the card shows additional content (if available). For some cards, there is the ability to set a 'reminder' on a card which will trigger a notification on the device at the time(s) you set, enabling you to open the card again quickly.

The following additional features are also available ...

Spotlight Search

SAP Mobile Cards integrates with Spotlight Search on the device (provided this has not been disabled by your Administrator). As new cards arrive, they will be indexed and become searchable using Spotlight search.



'Here and Now'

To find all cards which relate to addresses close to your current location, or have time-based attributes that are due to take place soon, use the 'shake' gesture on the device. To do this, open the SAP Mobile Cards app, and shake the phone. Any cards that fall into these categories will automatically be displayed in the search results.

Fiori Launchpad Users

In order to push content to your deck, open your Fiori launchpad, navigate to the item of interest, and then (from the Fiori 'me' area) click the 'Add to Mobile' icon ...
